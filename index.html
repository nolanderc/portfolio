Voxel Raytracer

This application realistically simulates how light interacts with an environment made up of only cubes. The programs utilizes GPU acceleration and a denoising algorithm in order to produce quality images in real-time, even on low-end hardware.

Postgres Query Library

Snow Fight

A silly multiplayer game where two snowmen have a fight. But instead of throwing snowballs, they throw regular objects such as trees and mushrooms! The challenge here was the creation of a custom network protocol that could handle packet-loss, spikes in latency, consistency between clients and more.
