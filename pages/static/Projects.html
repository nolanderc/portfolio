Real-Time Voxel Ray Tracing

Display ray traced voxel environments in real-time.

Complex voxel environments can be dynamically loaded onto a GPU and be ray-traced in real-time thanks to an efficient Octree traversal algorithm of my design.

There's currently support for voxels of different colors, camera movement and shadows!
